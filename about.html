About us

Built for service to humanity.

The foundation exists to reach out to widows and less privileged people with substance, love and hope for a fulfilled life.

Brief history

Conceived in December 2020, registered on 22 July 2021.

The vision of Olaidefaith Global Foundation was conceived in December 2020 by Chaplain Olaidefaith.

The NGO was formally registered on the 22nd July 2021. It is a non-governmental, non-political, non-religious, non-ethnic and non-profit sharing organization.

The founder of the organization is a passionate fellow who derives joy from empowering widows, less privileged and the downtrodden through free skill acquisition and various forms of empowerment.

Mission

To reach people with substance, love and hope.

To reach out to widows and less privileged people with our substance, love and hope for a fulfilled life.
