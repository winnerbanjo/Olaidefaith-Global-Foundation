Programs and services

Relief today. Skills for tomorrow.

Our programs address hunger, empowerment, learning and care for widows, young people, girls, children and less privileged people.

Main programs

Four pillars of practical service.

Women and Youth Empowerment

The foundation supports women, widows and young people with encouragement, care and empowerment activities that help families stand stronger.

Free Skill Acquisition

Free skill acquisition training and empowerment projects run twice a year. The recommendation is to enlarge this scope so more persons can be empowered.

Food Aid

Food Aid is done twice a year. It takes care of the hungry in our communities. The project has been recommended for wider outreach to include all classes of persons because of economic hardship in Nigeria.

Girl Child Day

International Girl Child Day activities celebrate, encourage and mentor girls through education-centered and dignity-centered outreach.
